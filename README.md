### About
Embedded Performance & Power Optimization Engineer with 3+ years of experience designing and optimizing real-time embedded systems. I specialize in improving system performance, power efficiency, and reliability across firmware and low-level software stacks — from DRAM/NVMe memory subsystems at Micron Technology to automotive ECU firmware at Infineon Technologies.

### Focus
- **Performance & Power:** DRAM power management, DFx/DFS, self-refresh states, firmware scheduling optimization
- **Memory & Storage:** DDR5, LPDDR, NAND Flash, NVMe/PCIe, SSD firmware, JEDEC standards
- **Automotive Embedded:** AUTOSAR (MCAL, RTE), FreeRTOS, CAN/LIN/SPI, ECU firmware, HIL testing
- **Tooling & Debug:** Perf, OProfile, LTTng, Trace32, Tracealyzer, JTAG, GDB, ETM/ITM tracing
